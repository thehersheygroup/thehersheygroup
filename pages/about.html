Our Story

25+ years of trusted custom manufacturing

Our team consists of seasoned professionals with over 60 years of combined experience. Our vision is to be a premier supplier in the promotional products industry. We have dedicated ourselves to being responsive, reliable, and offering high-quality products at competitive prices.

Celebrating over 25 years in business, The Hershey Group is a full-service direct manufacturer of custom promotional products. Specializing in our industry's top-selling product categories, we can source and deliver most any product for you.

We oversee quality control, product safety, CPSIA compliance, and the many details related to custom manufacturing — without you needing to worry about any of it.
